About Me

With over 20 years of experience, I have evolved from a 3D artist into the lead developer of Zen UV — one of the most advanced and widely used UV toolkits for Blender.

My deep understanding of modeling and production processes helps me create tools that are both powerful and artist-friendly. I’m part of the Zen Masters team, where we develop tools that improve the daily workflow of thousands of Blender users.

I’ve worked as a 3D artist on various projects with Red Dot Studio and contributed track modeling for racing games developed by Milestone.
